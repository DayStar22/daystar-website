The Expert Behind Your Report

Every DayStar assessment is built on years of cultivation research, consulting practice, and published expertise.

LF

Lance Fisher

Author • Cannabis Cultivation Consultant • Cannabis Educator

Founder of DayStar Cannabis Education & Consulting

I've spent years working at the intersection of cannabis science, cultivation practice, and grower education. My background isn't in marketing cannabis — it's in understanding it at a biochemical and agronomic level, and translating that understanding into tools growers can actually use. The foundation of that work is my book, The Great Cannabinoid Disconnect, which examines the gap between what the plant is capable of producing and what most cultivation environments actually allow it to express. That disconnect is the core problem DayStar was built to solve.

Before building the Grow Assessment, I consulted with growers across the scale spectrum — from single-tent home operations to multi-room commercial facilities. What I found consistently was the same pattern: growers who were technically proficient in one or two areas, blind to gaps in two or three others, and operating without any structured benchmark to tell them the difference. The DayStar Grow Assessment is my answer to that. It applies the same rubric I use in paid engagements — scored, ranked, and delivered in a format any grower can act on within 24 hours of submission.
